About Me

I am a full-time student in Thinkful’s boot camp learning front end web design.

I worked for a print and bindery company and I held a few positions in the plant, ending up in IT/Mailing. I started designing Access Databases and fell in love with VBA.

Before I was assigned to the IT/Mailing office I worked as the shipping clerk. It was there as I dissected the shipping database that I began to learn VBA code. I fell in love with the power and ability of the code.

Being a very logical minded person makes web development very satisfying to me. I like a challenge and solving problems. More than that I like helping people and learning new ways to do that.

Out of the office you can find me drawing portraits, cooking, or trying to help those around me.
